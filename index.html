The Akan Naming System

This a website that is used to determine the an individuals name based on their day of birth.
An individual is prompted to enter their birth details then according
to that one is able to know his/her Akan Name If you are male you will be named as:

      Sunday: Kwasi
      Monday: Kwadwo
      Tuesday: Kwabena
      Wednesday: Kwaku
      Thursday: Yaw
      Friday: Kofi
    Saturday: Kwame

If you are male you will be named as follows:

      Sunday: Akosua
      Monday: Adwoa
      Tuesday: Abenaa
      Wednesday: Akua
      Thursday: Yaa
      Friday: Afua
      Saturday: Ama
